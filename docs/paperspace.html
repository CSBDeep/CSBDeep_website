How to use Paperspace to test CARE networks


    Click on the Paperspace icon to start a preconfigured Ubuntu workstation that will allow you to try CSBDeep from within Fiji and KNIME. Note, only authorized users (e.g. reviewers) are granted access to this workstation.

    1. Log into paperspace.com using the provided account.
    2. Click the paperspace icon on the right.
    3. Click on the white circle in the top right corner and choose "Start machine".
    4. Wait until a desktop shows up (this may take a while).
    5. After testing, please shutdown the machine (again via the circle on the top right corner).
